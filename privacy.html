Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2025

At Eventus, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes data like your name, email address, and payment information provided during registration or transactions.
  • Usage Data: This includes information about how you use our website, such as pages visited, links clicked, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience and gather analytics to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal data may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services, including event listings and ticket purchases.
  • To send you promotional materials or updates regarding events you may be interested in.
  • To improve the functionality of the website and ensure it meets your needs.
  • To process transactions and manage payments securely.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. This includes encryption, secure servers, and limited access to sensitive data.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners for the purpose of providing services, such as payment processing and event management.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access and update your information.
  • The right to request the deletion of your data.
  • The right to opt-out of marketing communications.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your information is used, please contact us at info@eventus.com.
